OPENHWY - THE 60-SECOND VERSION

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE PROBLEM:
Autonomous trucks will destroy 3.5M truck driver jobs in the next 10 years.
But they can't navigate cities - only highways.

THE INSIGHT:
The "last-mile" (highway exit → final destination) still needs humans.
But if Big Tech builds proprietary platforms, drivers become exploited gig workers.

THE SOLUTION:
OpenHWY - an open protocol (like HTTP or email) for autonomous truck → human 
driver handoffs.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Autonomous truck reaches city
2. OpenHWY broadcasts job to licensed drivers
3. Driver picks up cargo at staging area
4. Completes last-mile delivery
5. Gets paid automatically via escrow

THE MAGIC:
- OpenHWY License = Blockchain-based driver credential (portable, can't be revoked)
- Works across ALL platforms (Uber Freight, DoorDash, whoever builds on it)
- Drivers OWN their reputation (not locked to one company)
- Open protocol = No monopoly possible

THE PROTECTION:
✓ Fair pay (escrow + smart contracts)
✓ Portable reputation (blockchain license)
✓ Network effects (more platforms = more driver power)
✓ Knowledge economy (get paid for sharing experience)
✓ Anti-exploitation (Whisper Witness detects broker manipulation)

THE BUSINESS MODEL:
- You're not THE platform, you're the PROTOCOL
- Like Linux: Build the first implementation (fedispatching.com)
- Release protocol as open source
- 70+ companies build on it
- Everyone competes at application layer
- Drivers win because infrastructure is open

THE TIMELINE:
2025-26: Build community (8teenwheelers.com) + tools (Wheeler Tools)
2027-28: Partner with first autonomous fleet (pilot program)
2029-30: Scale to 100,000+ drivers across all platforms
2031+: OpenHWY becomes THE standard for autonomous last-mile

THE OUTCOME:
Instead of: "3.5M drivers lost jobs"
Result: "3.5M drivers became last-mile specialists with portable credentials, 
fair wages, and ownership of their reputation"

NOT A STARTUP. INFRASTRUCTURE.
NOT A PLATFORM. A PROTOCOL.
NOT A MONOPOLY. A MOVEMENT.

The highway for the autonomous age.
Built by drivers, for drivers.
